Elden is a Ph.D. student at the University of Sydney, under the supervision of Archil Kobakhidze. He studies theoretical high-energy physics, with his principle interests lying in non-perturbative effects in gauge field theories, quantum gravity and their interface on the event horizons of black holes.

Currently Elden is thinking about:

  • Electromagnetic instantons around Euclidean black holes
  • Topology and edge modes of calorons, periodic instanton in quantum chromodynamics at non-zero temperature.
  • Regularisation and gauge anomaly.
  • Dualities in higher-form gauge theories.
